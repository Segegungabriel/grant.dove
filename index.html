About Grant Dove

Established in 2018 in Wellington, New Zealand, Grant Dove is a pioneering grant-making organization committed to fostering innovation and community resilience. With a mission to bridge opportunity gaps, we’ve disbursed over NZ$250,000 to 75+ projects across 12 countries, supporting everything from renewable energy startups to cultural preservation efforts.

Our approach combines rigorous evaluation with a human touch, ensuring every dollar creates measurable impact. Recognized by the NZ Philanthropy Council in 2023, we’re now taking our vision global — starting with Nigeria.

On Its Way to Nigeria

In Q3 2025, Grant Dove is launching its boldest chapter yet: bringing our grant-making expertise to Nigeria. Why Nigeria? With its vibrant population of over 200 million, a thriving entrepreneurial spirit, and untapped potential, it’s the perfect place to amplify our impact.

Our vision is to fund 50 projects in our first year, starting in Lagos and Abuja. We’re targeting:

  • Renewable energy solutions for rural communities.
  • Youth-led tech innovations in fintech and agritech.
  • Cultural festivals celebrating Nigeria’s rich heritage.

We’ve partnered with local organizations like the Lagos Impact Hub and are setting up a field office in Ikeja by July 2025. Our goal? Invest NZ$100,000 in Nigeria’s future by 2026, creating a ripple effect of opportunity across West Africa.
