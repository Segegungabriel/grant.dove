About Grant Dove

Since our founding in Wellington, New Zealand, in 2018, Grant Dove has been a beacon of hope for innovators and communities worldwide. We’ve awarded NZ$300,000 across 85 projects in 15 countries, from solar farms in Fiji to literacy programs in India. Our mission is simple: fund bold ideas that create lasting change.

In 2024, we were honored with the NZ Social Impact Award for our transparent, community-driven approach. Now, we’re setting our sights on Nigeria, where we see unparalleled potential to drive progress.

Launching in Nigeria

In July 2025, Grant Dove will land in Nigeria, kicking off a transformative journey in Africa’s most dynamic nation. Nigeria’s 200 million-strong population, thriving tech hubs, and rich cultural tapestry make it the ideal launchpad for our global expansion.

We’re committing NZ$150,000 to fund 60 projects in our first year, starting in Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt. Our focus areas include:

  • Solar-powered microgrids for off-grid villages.
  • Fintech startups tackling financial inclusion.
  • Cultural initiatives like Nollywood workshops and Yoruba music festivals.

We’re collaborating with Lagos-based Venture Garden Group and plan to open a permanent office in Victoria Island by September 2025. Our vision is to empower Nigeria’s next generation of leaders, creating a model for West Africa and beyond.
